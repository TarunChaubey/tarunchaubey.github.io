About Me

I'm Tarun Chaubey, a B.Tech. Computer Science graduate working as a Data Scientist - Gen AI & Computer Vision at Ascentt AITek Pvt Ltd, where I design and implement AI-powered solutions across domains like Automobile, Manufacturing, and BFSI. My core focus is on building scalable Generative AI and Computer Vision systems for Compliance Management, HSE, Tracking & Monitoring, and R&D Data Enrichment, leveraging SOTA open-source models along with APIs from OpenAI and Gemini. These solutions are deployed both on AWS and Azure cloud platforms, often integrating with on-premise systems to ensure seamless operation. Previously, as an Associate Data Scientist at Celebal Technology, I was responsible for translating client objectives into technical architectures, developing full pipelines from data ingestion to model deployment. I built end-to-end SAAS platforms, including a drone-based anomaly detection system for overhead line towers and a solar panel inspection system using RTPS frames. In the Generative AI domain, I developed tools like EKA and post-call analytics platforms that extract insights from audio and text, including NER, sentiment analysis, key phrase extraction, and CSAT scoring. My technical stack includes Python, ML/DL, NLP, OpenAI, Gemini, and MLOps tools such as Docker, Kubernetes, Git, alongside Power BI and Tableau for visualization. I also collaborate with infrastructure teams to ensure secure and efficient deployment in both public and client cloud environments.
